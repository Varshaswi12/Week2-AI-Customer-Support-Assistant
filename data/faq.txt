Welcome to TechNova Electronics Customer Support.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What are your customer support hours?
Our customer support team is available Monday to Saturday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM IST.

2. How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping takes 3–5 business days. Express shipping takes 1–2 business days.

3. Can I track my order?
Yes. Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking link via email and SMS.

4. What is your return policy?
Products can be returned within 30 days of delivery if they are unused and in their original packaging.

5. How long do refunds take?
Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after the returned product passes inspection.

6. Can I cancel my order?
Yes. Orders can be cancelled before they are shipped.

7. What payment methods do you accept?
We accept UPI, Credit Cards, Debit Cards, Net Banking, and Cash on Delivery (for eligible locations).

8. Is Cash on Delivery available?
Yes, Cash on Delivery is available in selected locations.

9. Do your products come with a warranty?
Yes. Most products include a 1-year manufacturer warranty unless otherwise specified.

10. How can I contact customer support?
You can email support@technova.com or call +91-9876543210 during business hours.
